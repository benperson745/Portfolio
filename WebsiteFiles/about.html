Hello!

My name is Benjamin Person

I am a game designer and programmer with a particular interest in telling stories through interlocking mechanical systems the same way many interactive stories have impacted me to want to explore user experience as a career. To accomplish this, I have created a number of individual and team projects where I have acted as an interdisciplinary go-between for different members of the team. The reason these projects were successful was in part due to my ability to break development problems down into manageable chunks and integrate each chunk step-by-step as well as communicate these breakdowns for team members with varying technical backgrounds (art, programming, UX, etc.). This ensured that each chunk worked on its own before being added onto by the next chunk. Additionally, the schedule and process for when and how tasks were accomplished were tracked in a number of documents I helped create and maintain.
